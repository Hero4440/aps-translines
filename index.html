APS Express Service

At times when speed is critical and your goods have to be delivered as fast as possible, we transport your goods through the fastest mode and ensure that your shipments reach the destinations as fast as possible

APS Warehouse Facility

APS Logistics takes maximum care of your goods right from the time we collect them to the time we deliver them, even when your goods are not in transit, you can be assured that they are safe in our care. For this we have well – equipped and well – manned warehouses with ample storage space for storing your precious cargo. Our warehouses are located at Pune, Bhiwandi, Mumbai, Kolhapur, Coimbatore, Chennai and Bangalore.

APS Network

For goods to be delivered at normal speed, our fleets of modern and fully equipped vehicles are at your service 24 x 7. Armed with a force of 200+ vehicles, 50+ centers and a network of hundreds of routes, we cover the states of entire India and our vehicles ply the entire length and breadth of our Nation.
